How It Works

Three steps. Zero missed calls.

No apps to learn. No tech to manage. Just more plumbing leads landing in your pocket.

01

Your phone forwards after 3–4 rings

If you're on a job and can't pick up, the call diverts to your RingCatch number. No hardware, no apps — just a simple call forward.

02

Your plumber agent has a real conversation

It answers like a receptionist who knows plumbing — asks about the problem, whether it's a leak or a boiler, the property type, urgency, and preferred callback times.

03

You get an SMS with everything you need

Within seconds: caller name, number, what's wrong, property type, and when they want it sorted. You call back informed, on your terms.
