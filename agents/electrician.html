How It Works

Three steps. Zero missed calls.

No apps to learn. No tech to manage. Just more electrical leads landing in your pocket.

01

Your phone forwards after 3–4 rings

If you're on a job and can't pick up, the call diverts to your RingCatch number. No hardware, no apps — just a simple call forward.

02

Your electrician agent has a real conversation

It answers like a receptionist who knows electrics — asks about the fault, whether it's a consumer unit issue or socket problem, the property type, and how urgent it is.

03

You get an SMS with everything you need

Within seconds: caller name, number, what's gone wrong, property type, and when they want it sorted. You call back informed, on your terms.
